Minecraft General Debug Shortcuts:
F3: Opens the debug screen, showing FPS, coordinates, chunk updates, and more.

F3 + C: Copies the player's location to the clipboard (useful for commands or sharing exact coordinates).

F3 + I: Copies block or entity data to the clipboard in JSON format.

Advanced Debugging:

F3 + Alt: Displays frame time graph (visualizes the performance per frame).
Chunk/Rendering Management:
F3 + C: Forces a crash (hold for 10 seconds).
F3 + F: Increases render distance by 1 chunk.
F3 + Shift + F: Decreases render distance by 1 chunk.

F3 + G: Show/hide chunk borders.
F3 + I: Copy entity/block data to clipboard.

HOW TO KEEP YOUR GOOGLE ACCOUNT ACTIVE
(To prevent deletion due to 2-year inactivity)

--------------------------------------------------
WHAT IS GOOGLE'S INACTIVE ACCOUNT POLICY?
--------------------------------------------------
Google may delete PERSONAL Google Accounts (like your @gmail.com) if they haven't been signed into or used for 2 YEARS.
This policy started enforcement on December 1, 2023.
This means the EARLIEST an account could be deleted under this rule is December 1, 2025.
Google WILL send multiple email warnings to the account and its recovery email (if set) MONTHS before any deletion.

This policy generally DOES NOT affect:
- Accounts through work, school, or other organizations.
- Accounts with active subscriptions (e.g., Google One, YouTube Premium).
- Accounts with YouTube channels that have uploaded videos.
- Accounts used to purchase digital items or that hold a gift card balance.

--------------------------------------------------
HOW TO KEEP YOUR GOOGLE ACCOUNT ACTIVE
--------------------------------------------------
You simply need to SIGN IN or USE your Google Account at least ONCE EVERY TWO YEARS.

Any of these actions count as activity (if you're signed in):
1.  Read or send an email in Gmail.
2.  Use Google Drive (open a file, upload something).
3.  Watch a YouTube video.
4.  Download an app from the Google Play Store.
5.  Use Google Search.
6.  Use "Sign in with Google" on a third-party website or app.
7.  View photos in Google Photos.

--------------------------------------------------
WHAT TO DO - KEY STEPS:
--------------------------------------------------
1.  SIGN IN: For each personal Google Account you want to keep, sign into it.
2.  PERFORM AN ACTION: Do one of the activities listed above.
3.  REPEAT: Do this at least once every 2 years for EACH account.
4.  SET A REMINDER: Put a yearly or 18-month reminder in your calendar for each account, e.g., "Log into my_email@gmail.com".
5.  CHECK RECOVERY INFO: Make sure your recovery email and phone number are up-to-date in your Google Account settings (myaccount.google.com -> Security). This is where Google sends warnings.

If you don't use an account and don't mind it being deleted, you don't need to do anything. Otherwise, a quick sign-in and click will keep it active.
